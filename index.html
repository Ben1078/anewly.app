Past You

Let your own experience lead.

Anewly remembers what you choose to save—so future suggestions can be grounded in your life, not generic advice.

  • See patterns without judgment. Revisit mood, energy, and the things that helped over time.
  • Keep the whole moment. Save a short note, a voice thought, or photos when they matter.
  • Fresh starts still count. Progress is evidence of showing up, never a score to defend.

Private by default

Your life stays yours.

Anewly is local-first. There is no Anewly account, no server sync, and no feed watching what you do. Your check-ins, notes, photos, and recordings stay on your iPhone unless you choose to export them.
