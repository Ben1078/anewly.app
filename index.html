01 The check-in

Start with what is true

Less motivation. More useful context.

Anewly does not ask you to become a different person before you can begin. It notices where your energy is, checks what cannot wait, and narrows the day from there.

  1. 01

    Check in honestly.

    Notice your mood and energy without needing to explain, improve, or perform them.

    Begin where you are
  2. 02

    Protect the essentials.

    Medication, food, pets, school, work—what matters gets checked before anything extra is offered.

    Reality comes first
  3. 03

    Choose one thing.

    Get one suggestion shaped by your energy, your context, and activities that helped on similar days.

    One, not a list

Past You

“Your own notes become evidence.”

Anewly onboarding

Generic advice cannot know what a walk, a shower, ten quiet minutes, or calling one specific person has meant in your life. Anewly can learn from the moments you choose to keep.

  • RememberSave a short note, voice thought, or photos with a check-in.
  • NoticeRevisit mood, energy, and what helped without turning your life into a score.
  • ChooseLet those real moments make the next suggestion more relevant.

Private by design

No account. No feed. No audience.

Your check-ins, notes, photos, and recordings stay on your iPhone. Anewly is local-first and does not need a server—or anyone watching—to be useful.
