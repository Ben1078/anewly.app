01 How it works

A quick check-in, then one suggestion.

Tell Anewly how you’re feeling and how much energy you have. It checks the essentials you set up and suggests one activity.

  1. 01

    Check in

    Choose your mood and energy level. Adding a note is optional.

  2. 02

    Cover the basics

    Anything you marked as essential comes first, whether that’s medication, food, pets, school, or work.

  3. 03

    See one activity

    Anewly uses today’s check-in and similar past check-ins to make a suggestion.

Past You

Keep a note for next time.

When something helps, save a note, photo, or voice memo with your check-in. On a similar day, Anewly can use it to make a better suggestion.

  • Save itAdd a note, photo, or recording to a check-in.
  • Look backRevisit how you felt and what you tried.

Privacy

Your data stays on your iPhone.

Your check-ins, notes, photos, and recordings stay on your device. No account is required, and nothing is synced to an Anewly server. You can export a complete archive at any time.
